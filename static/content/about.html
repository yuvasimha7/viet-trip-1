🎬 Thailand-Vietnam Adventure: Season 2

After the chaotic cliffhanger of our Goa series in Summer 2024 — featuring missed buses, budget overflows, and dramatic sunburns — the producers proudly present the much-anticipated sequel: Season 2: Winter Reloaded.

This time, we're trading beaches for temples, coconut water for pho, and confusion for... well, more confusion. Set to premiere this November 2025, our squad is back and slightly more prepared (we hope) for an epic winter adventure through Thailand and Vietnam!

🎯 Plot Highlights

  • 🚠 Fansipan Mountain: Because climbing stairs at sea level was just too easy.
  • 🏞️ Cat Cat Village: Where the cats are mythical and the views are worth the cardio.
  • 🏃‍♂️ Alpine Coaster: Rollercoaster meets hiking — scream and sweat at the same time.
  • 🐦 Bird Park (Ninh Binh): Where Naresh tried to befriend a duck and failed.
  • 🏯 Bai Dinh Pagoda: So many steps, even our budget tracker gave up.
  • 🚣 Tam Coc: Magical caves, but also manual leg workouts disguised as boat rides.
  • 🏛️ Temple of Literature: Where we pretended to be intellectuals for Instagram.
  • 🛍️ Night Market (Hanoi): Where Pavan bought 7 T-shirts for ₹300 and still complained.
  • 🌆 Walking Street (Pattaya): The place where our "budget" met its villain.
  • 🏝️ Ko Lan Island: Aka “Koh Lanta” of lost slippers and questionable sunscreen.
